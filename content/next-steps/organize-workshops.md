# Host AI Safety Workshops 🎓

*Effort Level: ●●●○○ (3 - 10 hours)*  
*Areas: Community, Technical, Policy*

## Ensuring AI goes well, is a task too big for any one organization

That’s why our approach centers on training a network of volunteers and facilitators to bring high-quality AI safety education to their local communities.

**You don’t need to be an expert in AI or AI safety** to facilitate our workshops. Our sessions are non-technical and introductory, designed to be led by people who are already a few steps into learning about AI safety and shared with audiences who are completely new. We provide the training, structure, and ongoing support you need to facilitate confidently.
