Superhuman AI might be coming soon.
And we aren't ready.

The AI Safety Awareness Foundation is a volunteer organization dedicated to raising awareness about modern AI, highlighting its benefits and risks, and letting the public know how they can help make sure it's safe. We run workshops focusing on educating the general public in the following three areas:

Awareness

Bridging the gap in awareness between frontier AI research and the general public and what risks exist with modern AI development

Skills

Equipping people with the tools and skills to participate in modern AI discussion and development

Social/Political Consciousness

Building the social/political consciousness needed to convince policymakers to take AI and AI safety seriously
